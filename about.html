About

The auditor's standard, on your side of the table.

Adele Royal has spent more than fifteen years working in accounting and finance roles supporting Alaska Native corporations, government contractors, tribal organizations, and privately held businesses. Through Northbridge Accounting & Consulting, she now brings that experience directly to organizations looking for organized, responsive, and compliance-focused financial support.

She began her career in public accounting with Big 4 accounting firm KPMG, where she worked on audits for Alaska Native corporations, government entities, and federally funded organizations. From there, she moved into accounting and finance leadership roles with Doyon, Limited and Gana-A'Yoo, Limited before becoming Controller at Alaska Aerospace Corporation, where her work includes financial reporting, audit coordination, internal controls, and federal contracting environments.

Her background includes experience with audit preparation, budgeting, reconciliations, internal controls, government contracting, and compliance-focused accounting operations. Having also owned and operated a business herself, Adele understands the importance of practical financial management, responsiveness, and clear communication.

Northbridge Accounting & Consulting provides accounting support, controller services, audit-readiness support, and financial operations assistance for tribal organizations, Alaska Native corporations, and federally funded entities. Audit and attestation services remain independent and are performed by licensed CPA firms.
