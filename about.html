About

The auditor's standard, on your side of the table.

Adele Royal spent her career on the audit side of federally funded organizations. Northbridge Accounting & Consulting exists to put that experience to work before the audit — keeping the books the way she once expected to receive them.

Adele began as an auditor at KPMG, where she audited Alaska Native Corporations and the complex, federally funded entities that operate within them. She went on to hold controller roles at Doyon, Limited; Gana-a'yoo, Limited; Doyon Drilling; and Alaska Aerospace — organizations where fund accounting, grant compliance, and audit scrutiny are routine, not exceptional.

That combination — auditor's eye, controller's hands — is the whole point of Northbridge Accounting & Consulting. The work is prepared by someone who knows exactly what an independent reviewer will look for, and builds the record to meet it.

Northbridge Accounting & Consulting provides accounting and audit-readiness only; attestation and the Single Audit remain with independent licensed firms — a separation kept by principle.

  • Former Auditor, KPMG — Alaska Native Corporations
  • Controller Doyon, Limited · Gana-a'yoo, Limited · Doyon Drilling · Alaska Aerospace
  • Experience 15+ years in federally funded & fund accounting

How she works

Calm, exact, and accountable to the record.

No theatrics, no overreach. Books kept current, questions answered before they are asked, and a clear line between the work she does and the audit she will never sign.
