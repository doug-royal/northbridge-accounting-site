Services

Front-end accounting, kept to the standard of the people who will review it.

A defined scope, delivered with discipline — for organizations under federal awards, and for private businesses that want the same standard applied to their books.

For federally funded organizations

Tribal governments, enterprises, and nonprofits under federal awards.

Where allowability, allocation, and audit scrutiny are routine — and the books have to prove it.

01

Bookkeeping & monthly close

Day-to-day recording, reconciliation, and a disciplined monthly close so the numbers are never more than a month behind reality.

02

Fund & grant accounting

Award-level tracking, restricted-fund segregation, and cost allocation handled with the fluency that federally funded organizations require.

03

Financial statement preparation

Statements prepared on the appropriate basis, with the supporting detail organized so that every figure traces cleanly to its source.

04

Audit-readiness preparation

Records, reconciliations, and schedules assembled so the independent audit confirms the work rather than untangling it — fewer findings, faster fieldwork.

Scope, stated plainly

What we deliberately leave to others.

The integrity of an audit depends on separation. We hold to it.

Northbridge Accounting & Consulting does not perform the Single Audit, does not provide attestation services, and does not write grants. Those engagements belong with independent firms.

When an audit is needed, we work alongside your independent auditor — never in their seat.

Engagements are scoped to the organization.

Engagements are scoped to the organization and priced as a fixed monthly engagement — not an hourly meter. Fee and scope follow the size and complexity of the work, agreed in writing before anything begins. The first step is a conversation.
